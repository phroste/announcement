Jeff Garlin co-stars and executive produces the critically-acclaimed HBO series "Curb Your Enthusiasm." Born and raised in Chicago and then South Florida, Garlin studied filmmaking and began performing stand-up comedy while at the University of Miami. He has toured the country as a stand-up and is an alumnus of Chicago's Second City Theatre.
