Susie Greene


Played by Susie Essman


Susie Essman plays the venomous Susie Greene in the critically-acclaimed HBO comedy series "Curb Your Enthusiasm." Susie (Greene, not Essman) is the no-nonsense wife of Larry David's rotund, cheerful agent Jeff Greene (Jeff Garlin). Susie divides her time between New York City and upstate New York where she lives with her husband, four step-children, and two dogs.
